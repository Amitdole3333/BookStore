Overview

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki is a personal finance book that contrasts the mindset of the rich and the poor, emphasizing financial education, investing, and building wealth through assets rather than relying on a paycheck.

Relevance
  • Financial Literacy – Teaches essential money management skills, including assets, liabilities, and investments.
  • Mindset Shift – Encourages thinking like the wealthy by focusing on financial independence rather than job security.
  • Entrepreneurship & Investing – Highlights the importance of passive income through real estate, stocks, and businesses.
